About Cleanzit

Modern garment care built on care, consistency, and accountability.

What is Cleanzit?

Cleanzit is a modern garment care studio built on the belief that clothes deserve thoughtful handling, not mass processing.

We specialize in dry cleaning, premium wet cleaning, and steam finishing, using defined processes for different fabrics rather than one-size-fits-all methods. Every garment is inspected, assessed, and processed based on its material, construction, and usage.

Cleanzit exists for customers who value care, consistency, and accountability over shortcuts and bargain pricing.
